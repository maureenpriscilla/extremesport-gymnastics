ABOUT US

Georgia Gymnastics Club is a registered charity and a designated ‘Centre of Excellence’ for gymnastics. Our aim is to provide a safe, effective and inclusive environment in which our members can participate in pre-school, general and artistic gymnastics activities under the guidance of appropriately qualified coaches.

We have a proud pedigree of achievement at county, regional, national and even international levels. Former and current members have competed successfully at the Commonwealth Games, World Student Games, Internationals and annual National Championships in team and individual events.

We provide gymnastics activities for a wide range of ages and abilities.
